The No profit Tier

The No profit Tier is perfect for individuals who want to contribute to your nonprofit's mission. As a Supporter, they'll receive basic benefits and stay connected with your organization.

  1. Newsletter Subscription: Regular updates on your nonprofit's activities and impact.
  2. Invitations to Events: Attend virtual or in-person events.
  3. Recognition: Acknowledgment on your website or in annual reports.
  4. Priority Referrals: Be recommended to potential clients and partners.
  5. Price: Free (or optional small donation).

Bronze Tier

Business Explorer Tier is ideal for businesses and entrepreneurs who want to explore the benefits of Chamber membership. As a Business Explorer, you'll gain access to essential resources and networking opportunities.

  1. Basic Resources: Access to foundational business guides, legal templates, and industry reports.
  2. Local Networking Events: Attend Chamber-hosted networking events and connect with fellow business owners.
  3. Business Directory Listing: Get listed in our online business directory.
  4. Price: $9.99/month.

Silver Tier

Business Trailblazer Tier is for businesses ready to take their involvement to the next level. As a Trailblazer, you'll enjoy additional perks and visibility within the business community.

  1. Access everything from the Business Explorer Tier.
  2. Exclusive Workshops: Participate in specialized workshops on topics like marketing, finance, and leadership.
  3. Featured Spotlight: Your business highlighted in our monthly newsletter and on social media.
  4. Priority Referrals: Be recommended to potential clients and partners.
  5. Price: $24.99/month.

Gold Tier

The Business Summit Elite Tier represents the pinnacle of Chamber membership. It's designed for established businesses and industry leaders seeking unparalleled access and recognition.

  1. All Business Trailblazer Benefits: Access all features from the Business Trailblazer Tier.
  2. Mastermind Sessions: Exclusive strategy sessions with industry experts.
  3. VIP Events: Invitations to high-profile business events and galas.
  4. Business Showcase: Featured prominently on our website and at Chamber events.
  5. Price: $49.99/month.
